## 🚨 ROOT CAUSE

**Problem:** Momentum 15-25% is TOO RESTRICTIVE
- Only **8.7%** of stocks have momentum in this range
- **Median momentum: 6.95%** - way below 15% threshold
- **75th percentile: 11.7%** - still below 15%

**Context:**
- Dec 20, 2025 might be a market consolidation period
- Most stocks have momentum < 15%
- Filtering out 87% of stocks is too aggressive
