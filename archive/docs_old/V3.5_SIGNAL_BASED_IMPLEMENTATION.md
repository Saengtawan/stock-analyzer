## 🆕 What Changed

### REMOVED (v3.4 Arbitrary Rules)
- ❌ **Time Stop**: Exit after 10 days if gain < 2%
  - **Problem**: No technical reason, purely time-based
  - **Result**: Exited stocks that might have recovered

- ❌ **Partial Exit**: Exit if peak 4%+ then reverse < 3%
  - **Problem**: Arbitrary percentage levels
  - **Result**: False exits on normal pullbacks

### ADDED (v3.5 Signal-Based Exits)

✅ **Signal 1: Breaking SMA20** (Day 5+)
- Exit when: Close < SMA20 for 2 consecutive days AND > 1% below
- Coverage: 100% of historical losers had this signal
- Backtest: 51 trades (14.3%), Avg exit: **-1.24%**, Day: **6**
- **Why**: Support level broken = trend changed

✅ **Signal 2: Weak RSI < 35** (Day 5+)
- Exit when: RSI drops below 35 (very weak momentum)
- Coverage: 100% of historical losers had RSI < 40
- Backtest: 1 trade (0.3%), Avg exit: -3.34%, Day: 5
- **Why**: Momentum completely lost

✅ **Signal 3: Lower Lows** (Day 7+)
- Exit when: Making lower lows (5-day lookback) AND gain < 2%
- Coverage: 98% of historical losers had this pattern
- Backtest: 34 trades (9.5%), Avg exit: **-0.71%**, Day: **8**
- **Why**: Downtrend confirmed = get out early

✅ **Signal 4: Failed Breakout** (Day 3+)
- Exit when: Peak 3%+ but reverses below +0.5%
- Coverage: 26% of historical losers (false breakouts)
- Backtest: 31 trades (8.7%), Avg exit: **-0.83%**, Day: **5**
- **Why**: Fake breakout = momentum trap

## 🎓 Philosophy: ROOT CAUSE Analysis

### The User's Critical Insight:
> "exit เร็วช้าต้องดูว่าทำไมถึงเป็นเช่นนั้นต้องหา root cause"
> "ต้องคิดเป็นเหตุเป็นผลซึ่งกันและกัน"

*Translation: Must find ROOT CAUSE for exits, think with CAUSE and EFFECT*

### Why This Matters:

**v3.4 Approach (WRONG):**
- Time Stop: "10 days" → Why 10? Why not 9 or 11?
- Partial Exit: "4% → 3%" → Why these numbers?
- **No fundamental reason!**

**v3.5 Approach (CORRECT):**
- SMA20 Break → Support broken = trend changed
- RSI < 35 → Momentum gone = weakness
- Lower Lows → Downtrend = get out
- Failed Breakout → Fake move = trap
- **Every signal has a ROOT CAUSE!**
