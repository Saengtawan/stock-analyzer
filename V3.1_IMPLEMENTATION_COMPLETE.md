### 2. Sector Rotation Integration

**Problem:** Not accounting for sector momentum
- Stock in cold sector (-10%) less likely to gain even with good signals
- Stock in hot sector (+10%) more likely to succeed

**Solution:** Created `SectorRotationDetector` class
- Tracks 11 broad sectors (XLK, XLV, XLF, etc.)
- Tracks 9 theme ETFs (Semiconductors, Cloud, Biotech, etc.)
- Calculates 30-day momentum for each
- Applies boost/penalty to composite scores

**Sector Classification:**
- 🔥 **Hot Sectors** (>+3% momentum): 1.1x - 1.2x boost
- ➡️  **Neutral** (-3% to +3%): 1.0x (no change)
- ❄️  **Cold Sectors** (<-3% momentum): 0.8x - 0.9x penalty

**Current Hot Sectors (Dec 2024):**
```
🔥 Silver:          +51.7% → 1.2x boost
🔥 Gold Miners:     +16.7% → 1.2x boost
🔥 Semiconductors:   +7.7% → 1.2x boost ⭐
🔥 Financials:       +7.6% → 1.2x boost
🔥 Materials:        +7.6% → 1.2x boost
```

**Smart Sector Matching:**
- Priority 1: Match to specific themes (Semiconductors, Biotech, etc.)
- Priority 2: Fall back to broad sectors (Technology, Healthcare, etc.)
- Uses Yahoo Finance `industry` field for granular matching

**Example - LRCX:**
```
Industry: "Semiconductor Equipment & Materials"
OLD Match: Technology (+2.7%, 1.0x) ❌
NEW Match: Semiconductors (+7.7%, 1.2x) ✅

OLD Score: 48.7 × 1.0 = 48.7
NEW Score: 48.7 × 1.2 = 58.4 (+20% improvement!) 🚀
```
