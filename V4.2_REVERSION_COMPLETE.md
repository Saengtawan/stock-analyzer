## 🎯 Key Decision Factors

### 1. Median Return is Most Important
- **Median reflects TYPICAL trade performance** (not influenced by outliers)
- v4.2: **+4.80% median** = typical trade makes good profit
- v5.2: +1.16% median = typical trade barely profitable
- v5.1: +2.39% median, but driven by ARWR outliers

### 2. Automated 2-1 Score was Misleading
Initial automated scoring favored v5.2 (2-1):
- ✅ v5.2 better win rate (+4.3%)
- ✅ v5.2 better avg return (+1.79%)
- ❌ v4.2 better median return (+3.64%)

**BUT**: Median return difference is HUGE (-3.64%)! This means:
- v5.2 may have more winners, but they're smaller winners
- v5.2 typical trade underperforms by **-3.64%**
- Higher win rate doesn't matter if typical profit is worse

### 3. v5.x Lessons Learned
**v5.0**: Too strict (4.3% pass rate, 0 stocks passed initially)
- Momentum 15-25% requirement filtered out good stocks

**v5.1**: Relaxed but outlier-dependent
- Momentum 10-25%, 52w >70%
- Performance +5.59% avg, but WITHOUT ARWR outliers: only +1.19%!
- Median +2.39% still worse than v4.2

**v5.2**: Further relaxed but worse median
- Momentum 8-30%, 52w >60%
- Better pass rate (7.4%) and win rate (52.2%)
- BUT median only +1.16% (worst of all!)

## 🔧 v4.2 Philosophy: ANTI-EXTENDED

### Core Principle
**Catch HEALTHY momentum, avoid EXHAUSTED moves**

### 7 Key Filters

1. **Momentum Filter**: NO minimum requirement, but reject >38%
   - ❌ v5.x: Required 10-25% (missed early moves)
   - ✅ v4.2: Accept ANY positive momentum <38%

2. **RSI Filter**: Reject extended stocks >72
   - ❌ v5.x: RSI <65 (too strict)
   - ✅ v4.2: RSI <72 (anti-extended focus)

3. **52w Position**: NO requirement
   - ❌ v5.x: Required >70% (missed early-stage moves)
   - ✅ v4.2: No requirement (can catch early momentum!)

4. **Volume**: Context-dependent (0.8-1.8x)
   - Same as v5.x

5. **Trend**: MA20 > MA50
   - Same as v5.x

6. **Stability**: 5d momentum > -8%
   - Same as v5.x

7. **Anti-Extended**: Combined check (momentum <38% AND RSI <72)

## 📈 v4.2 Advantages

1. **Better Typical Performance**: +4.80% median
2. **More Consistent**: Not dependent on outliers
3. **Catches Early Moves**: No 52w position requirement
4. **Flexible Momentum**: Accepts 0-38% (vs v5.x's 10-25%)
5. **Similar Pass Rate**: 7.5% vs v5.2's 7.4%

## 🎯 v4.2 Scoring System (Updated)

Total: 100 points

1. **Momentum (40 points)** - Anti-Extended Focus
   - 15-30%: 40 points (sweet spot)
   - 10-15%: 38 points (healthy early)
   - 5-10%: 35 points (early stage)
   - 30-38%: 20 points (getting extended)
   - >38%: 5 points (rejected by gates)

2. **Volume (25 points)** - Context-Dependent
   - High momentum (>20%): 1.2-2.0x = 25 points
   - Moderate momentum: 1.0-1.8x = 25 points

3. **RSI (20 points)** - Anti-Extended Focus
   - 50-60: 20 points (neutral)
   - 45-50 or 60-65: 18 points
   - 40-45 or 65-72: 12 points
   - >72: 3 points (extended)

4. **Trend Confirmation (15 points)**
   - MA20 vs MA50: up to 8 points
   - 5d momentum: up to 7 points
